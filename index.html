About me

I am 18 years old and currently studying computer science in New Zealand, coding is one of my hobbies and one day I hope to turn it into a career. I first learnt Java around 5 years ago, Just over 4 years ago I got into the spigot API and have been making plugins ever since. I really started to make plugins as commissions/for clients during the start of 2020 when everything shut down and I had a lot of free time on my hands. I am profishent with GIT, Intellij, Gradle, and other software useful for development. One of my biggest strengths is the ability to overcome problems and adapt to solve them. I pride myself on my fast learning skills and my already wide knowledge. People around me describe me as: a punctual, hard working person who always gets the work done.
